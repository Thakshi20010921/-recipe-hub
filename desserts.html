Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Mousse

  • Ingredients: Dark chocolate, eggs, sugar, heavy cream
  • Steps: Melt chocolate, whip egg whites and cream separately, fold together, chill for 2 hours.

Fruit Custard

  • Ingredients: Milk, custard powder, sugar, mixed fruits (banana, apple, grapes)
  • Steps: Boil milk, mix custard powder with cold milk, add to boiling milk, stir until thick, cool and add fruits.
