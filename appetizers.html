Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

  • Ingredients: Baguette slices, tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil
  • Steps: Toast bread, mix chopped tomatoes with garlic and basil, top slices, drizzle with oil

Stuffed Mushrooms

  • Ingredients: Mushrooms, cream cheese, garlic, breadcrumbs, parsley
  • Steps: Remove stems, mix filling, stuff mushrooms, bake at 180°C for 20 mins
